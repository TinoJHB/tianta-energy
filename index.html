About Us

Energy Independence
starts with us

As a third generation family owned and operated business, Rainbow Solar & Electric is driven by an unparalleled understanding of the roofing, solar, and battery needs of today’s homes. We’re your trusted partner in sustainable energy solutions. With a passion for innovation and a commitment to excellence, we’re dedicated to transforming homes across Gauteng into energy-efficient and eco-friendly spaces.

Our Vision

To lead the charge towards a sustainable future by empowering homes with efficient solar and battery solutions, ensuring a cleaner and greener environment for generations to come.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing top-quality solar installations and home battery systems, coupled with exceptional customer service and personalized solutions, to enable homeowners across Gauteng to reduce their carbon footprint and achieve energy independence.
